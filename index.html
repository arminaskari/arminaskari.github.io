Armin Askari

 

I am a Senior Member of Research Staff at The Voleon Group. I received my Ph.D in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at UC Berkeley in 2022 where I was advised by Laurent El Ghaoui. Prior to my PhD, I received a B.A. in Applied Mathematics and B.S.c in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley, where I was fortunate to work with the late Pravin Varaiya and the late Andy Packard. I had also spent some time working at the University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies with David Zingg.
