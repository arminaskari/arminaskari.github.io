I am a third year Ph.D student in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at UC Berkeley advised by Laurent El Ghaoui. Prior to my PhD, I received a B.A. in Applied Mathematics and B.S.c in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley, where I was fortunate to work with Pravin Varaiya and the late Andy Packard. I had also spent some time working at the University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies with David Zingg.

You can find a link to my resume here.

Research Interests

My research interests are broadly in optimization, machine learning and control theory. Currently, I'm particularly interested in the following topics:

  • Design of efficient/scalable algorithms for large scale feature selection

  • Robust optimization, out-of-sample generalization and domain adaptation
